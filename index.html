About Me

I’m Gunashree Channakeshava, a Software Developer with a strong background in full-stack development, data-driven systems, and applied machine learning. I hold a Master’s degree in Computer Science from the University of Illinois Chicago and currently work as a Research Assistant in the Macroscale Ecology and Conservation Laboratory (MEC Lab). My work focuses on building scalable analytical pipelines, integrating large multi-source datasets, and applying statistical and machine learning models to real-world problems. I have hands-on experience with MEGAN (Model of Emissions of Gases and Aerosols from Nature), mixed-effects modeling, multivariate analysis, and XGBoost-based prediction.

Previously, I worked as a Software Developer Intern at Nano Kernel Ltd, where I built Spring Boot microservices, RESTful APIs, and responsive Angular-based web applications. I enjoy designing efficient, maintainable systems and translating complex data into meaningful insights. I am actively seeking full-time roles where I can contribute as a Software Developer across full-stack, data, and ML-driven projects.
